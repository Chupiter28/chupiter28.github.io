Common Questions

FAQ

Is it really free?
Yes. The free tier is free forever — no credit card, no hidden fees. We take a 5% fee only when you earn money through fan subscriptions, and 8% on digital product sales. If you earn nothing, you pay nothing.
How is this different from Linktree or Beacons?
Linktree lists links. Beacons adds a store. ERND is built specifically for musicians — with Spotify embeds, release banners, tour dates, fan subscription tiers, and a brand deal marketplace all on one page. Plus, we're the only platform actively built for artists in West Africa, Latin America, and Southeast Asia from day one.
When does the money hit my account?
Fan subscription revenue and product sales are processed by Stripe and typically hit your account within 2 business days. We never hold your money or use a credit system — it's real payments, directly to you.
What is the Founding Member deal?
The first 100 artists who sign up get the Momentum tier (normally $12/month) for life at $2/month. That price is locked forever — it will never increase, even when we raise prices for everyone else. 43 spots remain as of today.
Does it work for artists outside the US?
Yes — that's exactly why we built ERND. We support payment rails including M-Pesa, Flutterwave, Paystack, PIX, Mercado Pago, GoPay, and GCash in addition to Stripe. If you're an artist in Lagos, São Paulo, or Manila, ERND works for you.
